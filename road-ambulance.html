What is a Basic Life Support (BLS) Ambulance Service in Mumbai?

  • Basic emergency medical care:

    BLS ambulances offer oxygen, first aid, and non-invasive monitoring for patients in stable condition while they are being moved.

  • Trained paramedic staff:

    They are staffed by trained personnel who deliver patient care, keep track of vital signs, and ensure safe transport to a hospital.

  • Essential medical equipment onboard:

    The ambulances are equipped with a stretcher, an oxygen tank, a pulse oximeter, a blood pressure monitor, and basic tools for trauma care.

  • Ideal for non-critical cases:

    They are used for hospital discharges, for cases of minor injury, for patient transfers, or for follow-up appointments.
